PODDAR HIGHLIGHTS

3rd Time Toppers in whole RAJASTHAN

This is why we proudly say that Poddar is known for its Quality Education. For three consecutive times, our MBA students have secured ranks in the Top 10 of Rajasthan Technical University Examinations, proving our commitment to excellence in management education.

Rajasthan's Biggest Fashion Runway Show

Poddar International College proudly organized its Annual Fashion Runway, where top celebrity models showcased garments designed by our students. This grand event also highlights students’ talents in marketing, design, and event production. Fashion Runway provides budding designers with an unparalleled platform to establish and express their unique identity. Through carefully curated runway presentations, students convey their creative vision, style, and aesthetic to the audience and potential buyers.

Only College to have COE of Apple Lab

Poddar is proud to be the first and only college in Rajasthan to establish a Centre of Excellence for Apple, in collaboration with SKLZ TECT LLP . This initiative ensures that all Poddar students will have the opportunity to pursue Apple-certified courses, along with access to exclusive internship and placement opportunities.

Startup Support

As Poddar remains committed to fostering job creators, we organized Rajasthan's Largest Startup Fest – InnoFest, a three-day fest at Rajasthan International Centre. The fest was inaugurated by Honorable Governor Shri Haribhau Kisanrao Bagade, with strong support from the government. Hosted on a global scale, the event saw participation from international companies like AJMS Global. Additionally, 10+ Shark Tank startups took part, competing for funding from investors in our exclusive 10-Minute Millionaire Challenge.
